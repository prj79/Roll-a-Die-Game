Instructions:

1. Set the winning score, and press NEW GAME. The winning score defaults to 100 if there is no input.
2. In each turn, a player can roll a die as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT score.
3. BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his CURRENT score is lost, and his turn is over.
4. A player can choose to HOLD which transfers his CURRENT score to his PERMANENT score.
5. The first player to have his PERMANENT score exceed the winning score wins!
